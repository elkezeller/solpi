Namhean 2022



Prep Day! We didn’t really sail but we launched the boat in the morning and docked the boat in the Ungchun harbor. In the afternoon, we did a quick practice session for tacking and downwind sailing. We were still short one person for the race the next day, so we didn’t put up the spinnaker yet. We came in around sunset, cleaned the boat, and headed out for some delicious duck dinner.

Day One of Sailing! It was an early morning with the skippers meeting at 8:30 and the first warning at 10:00. We had a total of 3 inshore races where we got 6th, 12th, and 12th place. It wasn’t our best performance, but we had fun and we knew our crew wasn’t super experienced. After the races, we had another skippers meeting for the next day and headed out for some finger-lickin' good chicken dinner.

Day Two of Sailing! We had a long day ahead of us with the departure at 5am. We had to motor to the start line, which was about 20nm away from the harbor. The start signal was at 8:30 and we had a coastal race from Yeosu to Tongyeong. It was a tough race but we gave it our all and ended up with a decent finish. We were pretty tired after the race, so we just had a relaxing evening onboard and called it an early night.

Day Three of Sailing! Unfortunately, the race was canceled due to bad weather. We were all pretty disappointed, but due to the bad weather there was no other option but to return back to Tongyeong.
